Interaction Design and Development Program (G103)

Interaction Design is an interdisciplinary field merging design skills. his program consists of coding, interface development,3D modeling and graphic designs. This program teaches you the principles of design and different design strategies that allow you to expand your knowledge in design. It is a 3 year program with an optional 7th semester.
